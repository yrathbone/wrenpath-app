Why WrenPath is different

Not a keyword game

We don't just tell you which words are missing. We ask whether you actually have the experience behind them — and only add what you confirm.

An honest coach, not a scorer

Grades are calibrated, not inflated. A stretch role gets an honest grade and real growth suggestions, not a false sense of readiness.

Free, because it should be

WrenPath is a non-profit. Career tools that gatekeep behind a paywall are part of the problem — this one isn't.
